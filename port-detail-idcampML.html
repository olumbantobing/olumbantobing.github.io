IDCamp 2022 Machine Learning Developer

Certification & course information

  • Organizer: Indosat Ooredoo Hutchison Digital Camp
  • Course date: May 2022 - Sept 2022

This program is part of Indosat Ooredoo Hutchison's Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) in the Digital Education pillar to build Indonesia as a Digital Nation. The IDCamp online training module was developed by Dicoding as Google Authorized Training Partners in Indonesia, in collaboration with Indosat Ooredoo Hutchison by utilizing use cases that are often encountered in the industrial world, especially telecommunications and digital, and strengthened by online mentoring methods. IDCamp 2022 Machine Learning Developer for beginners consists of 3 parts, namely Belajar Dasar Visualisasi Data, Memulai Pemrograman dengan Python, and Belajar Machine Learning untuk Pemula.

  • Belajar Dasar Visualisasi Data: www.dicoding.com/certificates/07Z6R8OY2PQR
  • Memulai Pemrograman dengan Python: www.dicoding.com/certificates/JLX1GJW52Z72
  • Belajar Machine Learning untuk Pemula: www.dicoding.com/certificates/RVZK6291EZD5
